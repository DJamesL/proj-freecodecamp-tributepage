Linus Torvalds

The mind behind Linux
Linus Torvalds in a rare interview with TED people.
  • 1969 Born in Helsinki, Finland. He was named after Linus the Peanut cartoon character.
  • 1988 to 1996 Attended University of Helsinki. Graduating with a masters degree in Computer Science.
  • 1989 Served 11-month mandatory military service.
  • 1990 Returned to college. First encounter with Unix. Presented his thesis titled: Linux: a Portable OS.
  • 1991 First public prototype release of Linux.
  • 1997 to 2003 Moved and worked in California. Then later moved to Oregon.
